Privacy

Your Brain is yours.

Just… is built on a simple principle: what you read and what you think belongs to you. This policy explains exactly what that means in practice.

Effective date: 1 January 2025  ·  Last updated: 28 May 2026

Overview

Just… ("the app", "we", "our") is a reading habit app developed by Ryland Dean. This Privacy Policy describes what information is collected when you use Just…, how it is used, and what choices you have.

The short version: your reading queue, your reflections, and your Brain are stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not collect or transmit your reading content or personal reflections to any server we operate.

Information we collect

Information stored on your device

The following data is created by you and stored locally on your device:

  • Links you add to your reading queue
  • Article content fetched for reading (cached temporarily)
  • Reflections you write or dictate
  • Your Brain entries (article title, date read, reflection text)
  • Your reading streak and history
  • App preferences and settings

This data is synced to your personal iCloud account (via CloudKit) if iCloud Drive is enabled on your device. Apple's iCloud privacy policy governs that sync. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Information collected automatically

Just… uses Apple's built-in analytics framework (App Store Connect Analytics) to collect aggregated, anonymised usage data — such as how many times the app is launched, which iOS version is in use, and whether a crash occurred. This data is not tied to you individually and cannot be used to identify you. You can opt out of this collection in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.

We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not read, access, or transmit your reflections or Brain entries to any server we operate.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers or serve ads.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Article content fetching

When you open a link, Just… sends an HTTP request to the URL you provided in order to fetch the article content. This request originates from your device and goes directly to the destination website — not through a proxy server we operate. The destination website may log your IP address or other standard request metadata per its own privacy policy.

Voice reflection

If you use voice-to-text in the reflect window, audio is processed on-device using iOS's built-in speech recognition. No audio is sent to our servers. On iOS 18 and later with Apple Intelligence enabled, transcription and contextual prompt generation both occur on-device using Apple's private compute infrastructure, subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Apple Health (HealthKit)

Just… includes an optional Activity Rings integration. When enabled in Settings, the app reads your daily Move, Exercise, and Stand ring progress from Apple Health to display a summary card alongside your reading queue. This is entirely opt-in — the integration is off by default and only activates after you grant permission.

Just… requests read-only access to activity summary data. It does not write any data to Apple Health. Your Health data is read on-device and is never transmitted to any server we operate. You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Just….

Purchases

Just… is free. There are no in-app purchases and no payment information is collected or processed by us.

Notifications

Just… sends only local notifications — they are generated on your device and never routed through a push notification server we operate. Notification permission is requested only after your reading streak reaches 3 days. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications → Just….

Children's privacy

Just… is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through the app, please contact us at the address below and we will take steps to delete it.

Data retention and deletion

Your data lives on your device and in your iCloud account. You can delete all app data by deleting the app from your device. To remove iCloud data, go to iOS Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Just… and delete the associated data there.

Because we do not maintain a server-side copy of your data, we cannot delete it on your behalf — but we also cannot access it.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users within the app. Your continued use of Just… after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Write to us at: privacy@rylanddean.com
