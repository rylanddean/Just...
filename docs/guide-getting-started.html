Guide 01

Getting
started.

From download to your first Brain entry. The whole loop in one walkthrough.

Before you begin

What you need.

Device iPhone or iPad
iOS version iOS 17 or later
Account None required
iCloud Optional. Enable iCloud Drive to back up your Brain across devices.
The loop

Your first read, step by step.

The whole app is one loop. These five steps cover everything.

01

Download Just… and complete onboarding.

Download from the App Store. On first launch, Just… walks you through the reading loop and asks you to choose a few interest categories. These seed your initial feed suggestions — you can add or remove feeds any time.

02

Add a link to your queue.

Tap the + button in the top-right corner of the Home tab. Paste a URL into the field that appears, then tap Add. The link appears in your reading queue immediately.

Faster: in Safari or any other app, tap the Share button and select Just… from the list. The link is added without opening Just… at all.

03

Tap the link to read it.

Tap any link card in the queue to open the reader. Just… fetches the article, strips images and ads, and displays the text in a clean reading view. Read at your own pace.

When you reach the bottom, pull up slightly to trigger the reflect window.

04

Write one thought. 60 seconds.

A writing window opens automatically. A prompt appears as placeholder text — it disappears the moment you start typing. The timer pauses while you write.

One sentence is enough. One word is enough. Tap Save when done, or Skip to move on without writing. Either way, an entry is created in your Brain.

05

Check your Brain.

Tap the Brain tab at the bottom of the screen. Your first entry is there — article title, your reflection (if you wrote one), and the date. Your Brain rank is now Curious. Every entry from here grows it. It never shrinks.

Adding links

Three ways to add a link.

Use whichever is fastest in the moment.

Fastest

Share Sheet

In Safari, tap the Share button and select Just… from the list. The link is added immediately, without opening the app. Works from any app that can share URLs.

In-app

Paste a link

Tap + in the top-right corner of the Home tab. Paste the URL into the field and tap Add.

From your feeds

The Digest

In the Digest tab, tap + next to any article to add it to your reading queue. Tap the article title to read it immediately from the Digest without queuing first.

Share Sheet not showing Just…

Tap More in the Share Sheet and scroll down to find Just…. Tap Edit Actions to pin it to the top of the list. This is a one-time step.
