Mission

Remove every barrier between a link and a kept thought.

Everyone saves links they never read. Pocket and Instapaper are digital graveyards. And even when people do read, they forget almost immediately — there is no mechanism to capture the thought you had mid-article before it disappears.

Reading apps optimise for saving. Just… optimises for reading and thinking. One link. One reflection. Every day. That is the whole thing.

How it works

The loop is five steps.
You only need to do the first one.

01 — Save

Add a link.

Share any URL from Safari or any other app. It goes straight into your queue.

02 — Strip

Images removed. Distractions gone.

Just… fetches the article and renders it in a clean reading view. No ads. No related content. No chrome.

03 — Read

Just read.

Playfair Display, warm background, nothing else. The reader disappears so the words can stay.

04 — Reflect

Write one thought. 60 seconds.

After each article, a writing window opens. One sentence is enough. The timer pauses while you type. Skip is always visible. The Brain remembers either way.

05 — Brain

Kept. Your Brain grows.

Every entry is stored. The Brain never shrinks. Over months and years it becomes a map of how your thinking has grown.

Positioning

Just… is one thing.

Clarity about what the app is not is more useful than a list of features.

Not a read-later app.

No archive. You read it or remove it. The queue is not a graveyard.

Not a note-taking app.

Reflections are brief and contextual. Just… is not a second brain — it is a first thought.

Not social.

No public Brains. No sharing prompts. No community challenges. Your reading is yours.

Free.

No charge. No subscription. No in-app purchases. The Brain belongs to the reader, not the platform.

The Brain

The Brain grows with every reflection. As it grows, it earns a new title. Not a number — a name. The long game.
