Description

How to describe Just…

Use this copy when writing about the app. Do not paraphrase the name — it is always "Just…" with the ellipsis. The ellipsis is not a typo.

Short description

Read one link. Reflect for 60 seconds. Watch your Brain grow.

Just… strips away images and distractions so you can focus on the words. After each article, a short writing window lets you capture the thought it left behind. Every entry grows your Brain — a personal knowledge base that ranks up as you read more.

Not a read-later app. A reading habit.

One-line pitch

Just… is a minimal iOS reading app that turns links into a daily reading ritual — and your reflections into a growing personal knowledge base called the Brain.

Positioning

What Just… is not.

Knowing what the app is not is more useful than a feature list. If a comparison comes up in coverage, these are the accurate framings.

Not Pocket No archive. You read it or remove it from the queue. Links are not saved for later — they are read now or removed.
Not Notion Reflections are brief and contextual, not a second brain. Just… is not a knowledge management system.
Not social No public Brains, no sharing, no community features of any kind.
Free No charge. No subscription. No in-app purchases. The Brain belongs to the reader, not the platform.
Not aggressive Streak loss is noted quietly. Notifications are opt-in, requested only after day 3. No dark patterns.
